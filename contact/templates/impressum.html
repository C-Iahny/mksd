Cultural event with MKSD: MUSIKABEND by MUSIKPROJECT MADAGASKAR

Save the date and be ready because it is going to be lit! The first edition of the MUSIKABEND by Musikproject Madagaskar is taking place in Bremen on 28th May 2022 with the participation of some of the most talented Malagasy singers and artist in Germany. Lead by the public figure Chef Rija, the squad includes Fredda, Tiji, Mirindra and Valerie. All known for their amazing voice and talent for singing. Particularly, Fredda and Tiji where famous through the Tv show PAZZAPA few years ago.

We, MKSD, support the initiative as promoting Culture is one of our main essence.

So we invite all of you to join and support this event with us!

Sei Dabei!
